EDITING TUTORIALS

Creating Eye-Catching Thumbnails

  1. Start with a high-contrast background (black and white works well)
  2. Add a clear, recognizable Gorilla Tag character as the focal point
  3. Use bold, easy-to-read text (limit to 3-4 words maximum)
  4. Add visual elements that convey the video's content (maps, cosmetics, etc.)
  5. Ensure thumbnail stands out in YouTube's crowded search results

Video Editing Best Practices

  1. Keep intro sequences short (under 10 seconds)
  2. Use jump cuts to maintain viewer engagement
  3. Add subtle background music that doesn't overpower gameplay audio
  4. Include clear call-to-actions for likes and subscriptions
  5. Use visual effects sparingly to emphasize key moments

Optimizing Videos for YouTube Algorithm

  1. Create compelling titles with relevant keywords
  2. Write detailed descriptions that include timestamps
  3. Use all available tags related to Gorilla Tag
  4. Design custom thumbnails that have high click-through rates
  5. Encourage viewer engagement through comments and community posts
